## 🎯 Quick Tips for Outreach Success

### 1. Research Before You Email
- Check their website for existing partnership programs
- Look at their social media for recent projects
- Find the right contact person (not just info@)

### 2. Personalize Every Email
- Mention specific models you like
- Reference recent news or projects
- Show you've done your homework

### 3. Follow Up Strategically
- Day 3: First follow-up
- Day 7: Second follow-up
- Day 14: Final follow-up
- Then move on (don't be annoying)

### 4. Track Your Outreach
Use a simple spreadsheet:
| Builder | Contact | Date Sent | Response | Status |
|---------|---------|-----------|----------|--------|
| Teacup | info@ | 2024-03-01 | No reply | Follow-up |
| Summit | sales@ | 2024-03-02 | Interested | Call scheduled |

### 5. Offer Value First
- Share their content on social media
- Link to them in your content
- Introduce them to other partners
