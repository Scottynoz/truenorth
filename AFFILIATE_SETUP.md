## 🔗 How to Create Amazon Affiliate Links

### Method 1: SiteStripe (Easiest)
1. Log into Amazon Associates
2. Browse to any product on Amazon
3. Use the SiteStripe toolbar at the top
4. Click "Text" or "Image" link
5. Copy the generated link

### Method 2: Product Links Tool
1. Go to Amazon Associates dashboard
2. Click "Product Links" → "Product Links"
3. Search for product name or ASIN
4. Click "Get Link"

### Method 3: Mobile App
1. Open Amazon app
2. Find product
3. Tap "Share" → "Copy Link"
4. Add your tag: `?tag=YOUR-ID-20`

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Use Pretty Links:** Create clean URLs like `truenorthtiny.ca/go/ecoflow`

2. **Track Everything:** Use UTM parameters:
   `?tag=YOUR-ID-20&utm_source=website&utm_medium=button&utm_campaign=solar`

3. **Disclose Properly:** Always disclose - it's the law and builds trust

4. **Focus on Value:** Recommend products you genuinely believe in

5. **Test Links Regularly:** Amazon products go out of stock - check monthly
